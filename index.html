Projects

1. Comparative buckling behavioral studies of various composite materials.

Conducted Buckling behavior of E-Glass, CFC, GFRP and S-glass. ANSYS structural software was used to conduct this buckling analysis of various composite material. The strength of this composite material may vary according to their fiber type, resin matrix, Fiber orientation & shapes of material. The material properties and number of layers along with the fiber orientations were used as a input to conduct the Linear bucking analysis of the different composite material. S-glass has the higher buckling strength than E- glass. The buckling strength of CFC is greater thana GFRP.

2. Conceptual design of hybrid unmanned vehicle for medical applications.

The main objective of this project was to design an Hybrid Unmanned vehicle for medical proposes in CATIA to carry 2 units of blood with medical supplies to where the humans can’t go. The hand calculation were done to determine the Take-off weight, Wing span, Length, endurance speed and other parameters. The final model of UAV was generated with the help of CATIA V5.

3. Multi objective optimizations of blended wing body UAV.

Blended wing body aircraft can save the fuel up to 27% compared to the commercial aircrafts. The main objective of the blended wing body UAV is to lower the RCS(Radar cross-section) and to reduce the drag. The calculations for the wing body configuration were calculated using standard research papers and the finalized model was generated using CATIA V5.
